The Japanese art of folding paper into decorative shapes and figures is called Origami. Some pictures of this art, are given below:




  • In modern usage, the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices, regardless of their culture of origin.
  • Origami is derived from two Japanese words: Ori (folded) and Kami (paper)
    Types of Origami Art:
  1. Action origami
  2. Modular origami
  3. Wet-folding
  4. Pureland origami
  5. Origami tessellations
  6. Kirigami
  7. Strip folding
